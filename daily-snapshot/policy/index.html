Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2025

Daily Snapshot is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Daily Snapshot is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Photos: Images you capture or select for your daily snapshots
  • Captions: Text descriptions you add to your photos
  • Dates and Times: When you create or modify snapshots
  • App Preferences: Your settings choices (theme, notification preferences, image quality)

2. How We Use Your Information

All your data is stored locally on your device. We use this information to:

  • Display your daily photo journal
  • Organize your photos in timeline and calendar views
  • Send you optional daily reminders
  • Create backup files when you choose to export your data

3. Data Storage

Important: All your photos and data are stored exclusively on your device. We do not upload, transmit, or store your data on any external servers or cloud services.

  • Photos are stored in your device's local storage
  • Captions and metadata are stored in a local database
  • Backup exports create ZIP files on your device that you control

4. Permissions

Daily Snapshot requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: To capture daily photos
  • Photo Library: To select existing photos and save captures
  • Notifications: To send optional daily reminders (only if enabled)
  • Storage: To save photos and create backups

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Daily Snapshot does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. Your data remains completely private and under your control.

6. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal information or photos to any third parties. The only way your data leaves your device is when you explicitly choose to:

  • Export a backup file
  • Share a photo using your device's native sharing features

7. Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • We recommend using your device's built-in security features (passcode, biometric lock)
  • Regular backups can be created to prevent data loss

8. Children's Privacy

Daily Snapshot does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app stores all data locally on the device and does not transmit data to external servers.

9. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual photos at any time within the app
  • Clear all data through the app settings
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all associated data from your device

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

11. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over:

  • Accessing your data at any time through the app
  • Modifying or deleting your photos and captions
  • Exporting your data via the backup feature
  • Permanently deleting all data by clearing the app or uninstalling
