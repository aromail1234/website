Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: BreatheFlow is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with privacy in mind - we don't collect, transmit, or share your personal data with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Local Data Storage

BreatheFlow stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Breathing Session Data: Date, time, breathing mode used, number of cycles completed, and session duration
  • App Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode) and other app configurations
  • Statistics: Aggregated data about your breathing practice (total sessions, streaks, etc.)

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device using secure storage. It is never transmitted to external servers or cloud services.

1.2 Data We DO NOT Collect

BreatheFlow does not collect, access, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Health data or integration with health apps
  • Network activity or browsing history

2. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your breathing session history and statistics
  • Calculate streaks and progress metrics
  • Maintain your app preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized breathing recommendations

Since all data remains on your device, we have no access to it and cannot use it for any other purpose.

3. Data Security

BreatheFlow employs industry-standard security measures:

  • Local Storage: All data is stored using iOS/Android secure storage mechanisms
  • No Cloud Sync: Your data never leaves your device
  • No Account Required: No login or authentication needed, eliminating password security risks
  • App Sandbox: Data is protected within the app's secure sandbox environment

4. Data Retention and Deletion

4.1 Data Retention

Your breathing session data and preferences are retained on your device until you:

  • Manually delete the app from your device
  • Clear the app's data through device settings
  • Reinstall the app (data is not preserved across installations)

4.2 Deleting Your Data

You have complete control over your data. To delete all data:

  • iOS: Delete the BreatheFlow app from your device
  • Android: Uninstall the app or clear app data in Settings → Apps → BreatheFlow → Storage → Clear Data

5. Third-Party Services

BreatheFlow does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates entirely offline and does not require internet connectivity for core functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

BreatheFlow is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, we comply with children's privacy protection regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and similar international laws.

7. International Users

BreatheFlow can be used anywhere in the world. Since no data is transmitted or stored on servers, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. All data remains on your local device regardless of your location.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification upon app update
  • Posting the updated policy on our website (if applicable)

Your continued use of BreatheFlow after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app's History screen
  • Deletion: You can delete the app at any time to remove all data
  • Portability: Since data is not cloud-synced, it remains portable on your device
  • No Tracking: We don't track you, so there's nothing to opt-out of

10. Compliance

BreatheFlow is designed to comply with major privacy regulations including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation - EU)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act - USA)
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act - USA)
  • PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection - Canada)

Since we don't collect personal data, most regulatory requirements don't apply, making BreatheFlow privacy-friendly by design.
