Privacy Policy

BreatheFlow

Last Updated: October 7, 2025

Introduction

BreatheFlow ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our breathing meditation application. We believe in transparency and your right to privacy.

Key Point: BreatheFlow is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information whatsoever.

Information We Do Not Collect

BreatheFlow does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic information
  • Contact lists or photos
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

Local Data Storage

All data generated by BreatheFlow is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Breathing session history and statistics
  • Session data (cycle counts, breath counts, duration)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Selected breathing modes and customizations
  • Theme preferences (light or dark mode)

Important: This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers, third parties, or any external services.

Third-Party Services

BreatheFlow does not integrate with, use, or share data with:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Any other third-party services

Internet Connectivity

BreatheFlow works completely offline. No internet connection is required to use any features of the app. The app does not make any network requests or communicate with external servers.

Children's Privacy

BreatheFlow does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages and does not contain any age-restricted content.

Data Security

Since no data leaves your device, your information is as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric authentication
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using official app stores for downloading and updating the app

Your Data Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • No account or registration is required to use BreatheFlow
  • You can clear app data through your device's settings at any time

App Permissions

BreatheFlow requests minimal permissions:

  • Haptic Feedback: To provide tactile feedback during breathing exercises
  • Local Storage: To save your session history and preferences on your device

BreatheFlow does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive device features.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be:

  • Reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Communicated through the app if significant changes are made
  • Effective immediately upon posting

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

App Store Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store requirements for iOS applications
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
