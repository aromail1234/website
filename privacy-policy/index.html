LoanVision Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: LoanVision is designed with privacy in mind. All loan calculations are performed locally on your device, and no personal financial data is transmitted to our servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

LoanVision processes the following information that you voluntarily enter:

  • Loan amounts and terms
  • Interest rates
  • Repayment preferences
  • Currency settings
  • App preferences and settings

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • App usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Crash reports (if you opt-in)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Perform Calculations: All loan calculations are processed locally on your device
  • Customize Experience: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to enhance functionality
  • Provide Support: Help resolve technical issues when contacted

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All financial calculations and personal data are stored locally on your device using:

  • iOS Keychain for sensitive data
  • Core Data for app preferences
  • UserDefaults for app settings

3.2 Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure iOS development practices
  • Regular security assessments
  • No transmission of sensitive financial data

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal financial data with third parties.

Limited data may be shared only in these circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize sharing
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Service Providers: Anonymized analytics to improve app performance

5. Third-Party Services

LoanVision may use the following third-party services:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues
  • App Store: For app distribution and updates

These services have their own privacy policies and do not receive your financial calculation data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the app
  • Delete: Remove all app data by deleting the app
  • Correct: Modify any incorrect information
  • Export: Export your calculations in PDF or CSV format
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and crash reporting

7. Children's Privacy

LoanVision is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Users

LoanVision is designed to work globally. All data processing occurs on your local device, regardless of your location. Currency formatting automatically adapts to your region settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes

10. Data Retention

Your data is retained:

  • On Device: Until you delete the app or clear app data
  • Analytics: Anonymized data for up to 24 months
  • Support: Communication records for up to 2 years

Contact Us

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

12. European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
Remember: You can always delete all your data by simply deleting the LoanVision app from your device. No personal financial information is stored on our servers.
